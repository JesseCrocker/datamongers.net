Avalanche Lab 1.0 is finally here. Lots of great new features:

  • Avalanche Lab Pro: A bunch of great new features available for a small price
  • iPad support
  • Voice Recognition: Dictate your snow profile - only in Avalanche Lab Pro
  • Save To Dropbox - only in Avalanche Lab Pro
  • All your observations, snow and avalanche now in one menu
  • Observation Summary, get a quick overview without having to look at a bunch of screens
  • Online Social Data Sharing
  • Online observation browser
  • New smart sorting of observations
  • New Snow Profile editor
  • Snow profile layers now entered with top or bottom location instead of thickness
  • Imporved avalanche mapping
  • Sight points for avalanche mapping, ski down one side of a path and map the points on the far side by sighting them
  • Lookup elevations for mapped avalanches when you get internet access
  • Import Snow Pilot files

Now go out and update, then upgrade to Avalanche Lab for a bunch of great features, and to show that you support Avalanche Lab and want to see it continue to be developed.
